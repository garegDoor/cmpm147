Experiment 3 - Alternate Worlds

Description

For this experiment I made both a dungeon generator and overworld generator.

Technical

The dungeon generator starts with a room and then branches off up to 4 other rooms connecting them with paths and placing chests in each branch room. The overworld generator uses noise with bitmasking to create a landscape and then draws transluscent rectangles above each tile of water to create a rippling wave affect over time.

Reflection

I am pretty happy with the results of the experiment and would've liked to add more if I had given myself more time, but I went right to the deadline on this one.
